🔒 Client-Side Processing: Breaking It Down

A lot of tool sites say they care about your privacy. Very few actually build for it. Here is the difference.

When you upload a PDF, image, or document to most online tool sites, that file travels from your device to a company server, gets processed there, and is returned to you. Your data has left your computer. What the site does with it after that is entirely up to them.

AxiomApe is built differently. Our tools run directly in your browser using JavaScript and modern Web APIs. When you use our Image Compressor, PDF tools, Text converters, or any other utility, the processing happens right on your device. The file never leaves. The text never leaves. We never see it. We never store it. There is nothing to breach, sell, or mishandle. What happens on your screen, stays on your screen. Our tools are built to work, not to track you.

The only data that ever reaches a server is standard web traffic information, the same log data any website receives when you visit a page. See our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.
