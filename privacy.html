Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2025  |  Applies to: axiomape.com

1 Overview

Welcome to AxiomApe.com ("AxiomApe," "we," "us," or "our"). We operate a free, browser-based toolkit at axiomape.com covering a wide range of utility tools for everyday use.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use that information, and the choices you have. Please read it carefully. By accessing or using AxiomApe.com, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Our core commitment: We do not require user accounts. We do not sell your personal information. Most of our tools process data entirely within your browser and never transmit it to our servers.

2 Information We Collect

We collect two categories of information:

Information you provide voluntarily

If you contact us via our contact form or by email, we collect the name, email address, and message content you provide. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, certain technical data is automatically collected by our web server and third-party analytics and advertising services. This includes Log Data and Cookie data described in the sections below.

Tool input data: The inputs you enter into our tools (text, numbers, files, etc.) are processed locally in your browser wherever technically possible. We do not log, transmit, or store this input data unless the specific tool requires a server-side process, in which case that tool will disclose this fact.

3 Log Data

Whenever you visit AxiomApe.com, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • The pages of our website you visit and the time spent on each page
  • The date and time of your request
  • Referring URL (the page you came from)
  • Operating system and device type
  • HTTP status codes and other diagnostic data

Log data is used for security monitoring, diagnosing technical issues, analyzing aggregate traffic patterns, and improving site performance. Log data is not linked to any personally identifiable information and is routinely deleted on a rolling basis.

4 Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser by websites you visit. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as web beacons and local storage) for the purposes described below.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics). Data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Advertising cookies: Placed by third-party advertising networks, including Google, to serve relevant advertisements. See Section 5 for full details.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings or choices (e.g., dark mode preference).

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some portions of our website may not function correctly. Most browsers also allow you to delete cookies already stored on your device.

5 Advertising & Google AdSense

AxiomApe.com is supported by advertising revenue. We use Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC ("Google"), to display advertisements on our website.

Google AdSense disclosure (required): Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to AxiomApe.com and/or other websites on the Internet.

Specifically, Google may use the following to serve personalized ads:

  • Cookies placed by Google on your browser when you visit AxiomApe.com or other websites
  • Your prior browsing history across websites that partner with Google
  • Device identifiers and IP address information
  • Inferences drawn from your browsing behavior to build interest categories

Google's advertising practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Google AdSense Program Policies. We encourage you to review those policies to understand how Google collects and uses information from sites that use its advertising services.

Please note: AxiomApe does not control the data collection or advertising practices of Google or other third-party ad networks. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external parties.

6 Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You have choices about how Google and other advertising partners use your data to show you personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings — manage how Google personalizes ads for you across all services
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out (aboutads.info)
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out

You can also prevent Google's collection and use of data by downloading and installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Please note that opting out of personalized advertising does not mean you will no longer see ads on AxiomApe.com — it means the ads you see will be less relevant to your interests, based on general contextual factors rather than your browsing history.

7 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve AxiomApe.com and its tools
  • To respond to inquiries or support requests you submit
  • To monitor and analyze usage trends and site performance
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or malicious activity
  • To serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense and other networks
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

We have no control over the content, privacy practices, or policies of third-party sites, and we accept no responsibility for them.

We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit. The presence of a link on AxiomApe.com does not constitute our endorsement of that site or its practices.

Third-party services we currently use include:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic analytics (Google LLC)

Each of these services is subject to its own terms and privacy policy.

9 Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure and codebase
  • Restricting access to personally identifiable information to only those who need it

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

10 Children's Privacy

AxiomApe.com is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@axiomape.com.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information from our servers.

11 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights with respect to your personal information. These may include:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to erasure: Request that we delete personal information we hold about you, where applicable.
  • Right to restriction: Request that we restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Right to object: Object to our processing of your data for direct marketing or other purposes.
  • Right to opt out of personalized advertising: See Section 6 for instructions.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@axiomape.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to provide more prominent notice (such as a banner notification on our website).
