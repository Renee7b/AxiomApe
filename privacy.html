1 Overview

AxiomApe ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website AxiomApe.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when you visit or use our Site.

By accessing or using AxiomApe.com, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree to these terms, please discontinue use of the Site.

Privacy at a glance: Most of our tools run entirely in your browser and do not transmit any data to our servers. The primary data we collect is standard server log data and information gathered by third-party advertising and analytics services, as described below.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URLs, and general browsing patterns on the Site.
  • Log Data: Technical information automatically sent by your browser when you visit the Site (see Section 3).
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: Small data files placed by us or third parties on your device (see Section 4).
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email or our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Tool Inputs (Local Processing Only): Many tools on this Site process data entirely within your browser. This data (e.g., text entered into a converter, an image uploaded to a resizer) is never sent to our servers and is not stored or retained by us.

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personally identifiable information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3 Log Data

When you visit AxiomApe.com, our web server automatically records information sent by your browser. This "Log Data" may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and device type
  • The pages of the Site you visited and the time spent on each page
  • The date and time of your visit
  • Referring website address (the site you came from)
  • Other standard server log information

Log data is used in aggregate form to understand how visitors use the Site, diagnose technical issues, and improve the overall experience. It is not used to identify individual users and is retained for a limited period before being automatically purged.

4 Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. We and our third-party partners use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as web beacons and pixels) to operate the Site and deliver relevant advertising.

The types of cookies used on this Site include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic site functions (e.g., remembering user preferences set within a tool session). These cannot be disabled without impairing Site functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with the Site in aggregate. We may use services such as Google Analytics for this purpose.
  • Advertising Cookies: Set by third-party advertising networks, including Google, to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history and interests (see Section 5).

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you disable cookies, some features of the Site may not function properly. Most browsers also allow you to manage your cookie settings through their privacy or settings menus.

5 Advertising & Google AdSense

AxiomApe.com uses Google AdSense, an advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements on the Site. Advertising allows us to offer all tools on AxiomApe.com free of charge.

Important (Required AdSense Disclosure): Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to AxiomApe.com and other websites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the internet.

Specifically, Google AdSense may:

  • Use the DoubleClick cookie to enable Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to AxiomApe.com and other sites
  • Use cookies and web beacons to compile information about your activity on this and other websites to provide you with targeted advertising
  • Share information with other Google advertising products and services

Opting Out of Personalized Advertising: You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Please note that opting out of personalized ads does not mean you will stop seeing advertisements. You will continue to see ads, but they will not be based on your browsing history and may be less relevant to your interests.

For more information on how Google collects and uses data in connection with advertising, please review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy and Google's advertising data usage policies at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

6 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve AxiomApe.com and its tools
  • To understand how visitors use the Site and which tools are most valuable
  • To diagnose technical problems and ensure reliability
  • To respond to your messages, questions, and support requests
  • To display advertisements through Google AdSense and similar services
  • To monitor for and prevent fraudulent, abusive, or unauthorized use of the Site
  • To comply with applicable laws and legal obligations

We do not use your information to build personal profiles for sale to data brokers, and we do not engage in automated decision-making that produces legal effects concerning you.

7 Third-Party Services & Links

AxiomApe.com may contain links to third-party websites, tools, or services that are not operated by us. These may have their own privacy practices.

We currently use or may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — display advertising. See Section 5.
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage statistics. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) — for loading fonts or libraries; these may log your IP address as part of standard CDN operations.

When you click a link to a third-party site, you leave AxiomApe.com and that site's own privacy policy will apply. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or data handling of any third-party websites.

8 Data Security

The security of your information is important to us. We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any data we hold against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Limiting access to any collected data to authorized personnel only
  • Regular review of our data collection and storage practices

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users in accordance with applicable law.

9 Children's Privacy

AxiomApe.com is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under the applicable age threshold without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@axiomape.com.

10 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising: Visit Google's Ads Settings or use your browser's cookie controls.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal. We honor such signals where technically feasible.
  • California Residents (CCPA): California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
  • EEA/UK Residents (GDPR): If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us at support@axiomape.com. We will respond within 30 days.
